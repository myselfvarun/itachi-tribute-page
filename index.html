Itachi Uchiha

うちは イタチ

Itachi Uchiha (Japanese: うちは イタチ, Hepburn: Uchiha Itachi) is a fictional character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto.

Integrity- Itachi teaches us that we should do what's right, even when no one is watching us or expecting us to do the right thing.

Here's some information & my thoughts about Itachi

  • Created by : Masashi Kishimoto
  • Nickname : clan killer itachi
  • One name is enough to learn a whole lot about life
  • The saddest part about him was that he never had a life of his own.
    He always lived for others , but we never saw him whining and complaining.
